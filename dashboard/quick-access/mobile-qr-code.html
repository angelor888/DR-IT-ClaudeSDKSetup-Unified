Mobile Installation Instructions:

  1. Scan the QR code above with your phone's camera
  2. Open the link in your browser
  3. For iPhone: Tap the Share button and select "Add to Home Screen"
  4. For Android: Tap the menu (3 dots) and select "Add to Home screen"
  5. Give it a name and tap "Add"
  6. The app icon will appear on your home screen!
